# Dashboard 4 – HR Analytics

## Overview
This dashboard analyses employee attrition patterns using the IBM HR Analytics dataset from Kaggle.

## Key Question
Which employee segments are most at risk of leaving, and what does the data suggest?

## Key Insights
- Overall attrition rate is 16%
- Sales department has the highest attrition
- Sales Representatives are the most at-risk job role
- Younger employees tend to have higher attrition rates

## Visuals
- KPI Cards – Total Employees, Attrition Rate, Avg Income, Avg Tenure
- Bar Chart – Attrition by Department
- Treemap – Attrition by Job Role
- Column Chart – Attrition by Age
- Scatter Chart – Job Satisfaction vs Performance

## Tools Used
Power BI Desktop, DAX, Power Query

## Dataset
IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition – Kaggle
